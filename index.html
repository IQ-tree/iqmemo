The fast, easy to use memo application

We introduce IQ Memo.


IQ Memo is a fast, easy-to-use memo program, which saves your memo in cookies. This preserves a no-login feature, which will make it fast, free, safe.

Hackers could not gain access to your note, because your memo will be saved in your browser. Just be careful of people who can use your computer.

IQ Memo also provides a passcode option. A passcode could keep your data at least safe for people who can use your computer.
